# Supplier Cybersecurity Policy

## 1. Supplier Identification
Subsystems treated as suppliers:
- CAN driver
- Gateways
- HMI
- OTA subsystem

## 2. Requirements
Suppliers must:
- Provide version information
- Provide security‑relevant documentation
- Notify of vulnerabilities
- Support forensic analysis

## 3. Supplier Risk Assessment
Risks documented in:
- supplier_risk_*.json (CSMS snapshots)

## 4. Monitoring
- Periodic review of supplier components
- Validation of security behavior
- Integration into risk assessment
