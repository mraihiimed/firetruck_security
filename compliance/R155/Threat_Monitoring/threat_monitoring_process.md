# Threat & Vulnerability Monitoring

## Monitoring Sources (§7.2.2.2(g))
- CVE database
- Automotive ISAC
- Internal logs from gateways and main ECU

## Forensic Data (§7.2.2.2(h))
- Security logs stored in /logs/security/
- Includes PGN, SA, timestamp, decision

## Continual Monitoring (§7.2.2.4)
- Applies to vehicles after registration
- Logs reviewed monthly

## Timely Mitigation (§7.2.2.3)
- Critical vulnerabilities patched within 30 days
